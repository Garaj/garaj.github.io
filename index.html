About Where is it

  • What is Garaj?

    Garaj is a coworking space for hackers, makers and tinkerers in Hisarustu, Istanbul. We love to create and hack software and hardware.

    Most of us are students at Bogazici University, but we come from all walks of life and share our knowledge and create together in this special place.

  • Garaj Talks!

    We organize biweekly Garaj Talks where we invite and listen hackers, designers and entrepreneurs. We also had privilege to invite and listen stories of people from other disciplines and encourage to do so.

    Reach us if you want to come and share your experience with us!

  • Garaj is free!

    Free as in speech and free as in beer! You do not have to pay anything for building things at Garaj, and this is a special place where we love to hear interesting ideas from creative people like you!

    Just come and let's build things together.
